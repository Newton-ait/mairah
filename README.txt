# PAKANYA COFFEE WEBSITE

## Project Overview
A complete 4-page responsive website for PAKANYA COFFEE business based in Mubende, Uganda.

## Website Structure
1. index.html - Homepage
2. menu.html - Products & Ordering
3. about.html - Company Story & Process
4. contact.html - Contact Information & Form

- ✅ Responsive design (mobile, tablet, desktop)
- ✅ WhatsApp ordering integration

- ✅ Interactive navigation
- ✅ Product showcase with pricing

- ✅ FAQ section

1. Upload all files to your web hosting
2. Replace placeholder images in `assets` folder
3. Update contact details if changed
4. Test on different devices
5. Share your website URL!
